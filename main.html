Rules

The rules of QPLOY are simple. Poison other players by using marks, small pieces of paper with an 'X', either in or close by their food.
  1. The "poison" must be at least 1cm by 1cm with a clearly drawn "x"
  2. The poison must be either on the food, or something directly touching the food.
  3. The target has to actually eat the poisoned food. If he or she sees it's poisoned before they eat it, they are safe
  4. You must inform your target when you successfuly killed them
  5. The game is played at all times, every where.
  6. You can die as many times as you like, it will just negatively impact your score
  7. Trust no one
